Get Involved

Volunteer at the Language Lab

One way to experience the spirit of Auroville and its sense of community & collaborative work is to volunteer at the Language Lab. Volunteering here can be a profoundly enriching experience — over the years, volunteers from across the globe have generously given their time and talents, contributing to the Lab’s growth in immeasurable ways.

Our volunteers come from diverse backgrounds with varied perspectives, which adds freshness and fosters a spirit of healthy knowledge-exchange. We are a space for growth, inclusivity, and shared learning.

Opportunities

Ways You Can Contribute

Teaching a language (long-term)

Managing our social media accounts

Organising our reference library

Assisting in research projects

Supporting publication projects

Documenting the trees planted at ALL

An enthusiasm for our work and a willingness to contribute effectively as part of our team are our only prerequisites. Write to us to find out more.
