Reminder about our free Evening Programs

The evening conversation sessions are a chance for everyone to practice speaking with native speakers and offer their time. Barring indicated exceptions, these sessions usually happen weekdays from 5–6 pm as follows:

  • Mondays: French with Isabelle (1st, 3rd and 5th week of the month)… with Coco and Gaspard (2nd and 4th week of the month) 5–6 pm
  • Wednesdays & Thursdays: Sanskrit chanting with Remesh 5:30–6:30 pm

Language, Listening
& Sustainable Living

We are constantly striving towards an integrated and holistic way of learning as well as living. Through the Alfred Tomatis Method, we help children with learning disabilities overcome barriers to learning by strengthening listening, communication, attention, and cognitive development, while advancing research in the field.
