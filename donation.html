Where It Goes

What Your Donation Supports

Your contribution goes directly to active projects that shape the Lab’s future. Here are the areas that currently need support most urgently.

Building

Complete Our Sustainable Building

Our building is a stellar example of climate-conscious construction — passive cooling towers, lime-based walls, probiotics, and zero air conditioning. We still need to complete 15 outlet shafts, roof tiling, flooring for the caféteria and Indian classroom, the north terrace, and the remaining cooling towers.

Tomatis

Sponsor a Child’s Tomatis Program

Cover the cost of the Tomatis listening program for a child who needs it but whose family cannot afford it. The method has transformed the lives of children with autism, dyslexia, developmental delays, and attention disorders. Your support directly enables a family in need.

Documentary

Help Us Tell Our Story

We are producing a short documentary film with Inlight Media, Auroville, to capture the spirit, the work, and the people of the Language Lab — the Tomatis centre, the building, the classrooms, and the community. Fundraising target: ₹2,72,500 / USD 3,035. Every donation to AVI USA is matched 100%.

Publications

Translate Our Book on Tomatis

The Auroville Language Lab has published a transformational work on the Alfred Tomatis Method in English. We are seeking support to translate it into multiple languages so the message reaches the whole world. Your contribution funds translation, editing, and printing.

From Resonance to Bonding

Our book explores the Alfred Tomatis Method — how sound shapes development, heals trauma, and unlocks human potential from the womb onward. A product of 21 years of listening therapy practice at Auroville.

Help us translate it and bring this knowledge to readers around the world.
