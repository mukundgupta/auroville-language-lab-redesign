About The Method

What is the Alfred Tomatis Method?

The Alfred Tomatis method is the outcome of Dr. Alfred Tomatis's lifetime of research and discovery in the field of human hearing and sound perception. After decades of exploration into the functioning of the ear, its relation to the nervous system and the psyche, Dr. Tomatis developed a sophisticated Listening Training using a device he invented — the Electronic Ear.

The Auroville Language Lab is the first centre in India to offer this transformative program. Also known as "Audio Psycho Phonology," the method re-trains the ear through a combination of exercising the muscles of the middle ear, direct cortical stimulation, and modifications in the way language is processed.

The result: new neural synapses, better flow of messages in the brain, and more harmonious coordination between different parts of the brain — better listening to yourself, to others, and to the world around you.

Who Can Benefit

What can Tomatis Listening Training do for you?

Dr. Tomatis's work underscores the vital role listening plays in every aspect of life. Over the years, this training has transformed millions of people across the globe — from children with developmental challenges to professional musicians and artists.

Children & Development

Children on the autism spectrum, those with developmental delays, dyslexia, attention deficit disorders, and other learning challenges.

Language Learners

An EU study shows the method can reduce the time to learn a language by half — acclimatising the ear to sounds, frequencies and cadences for native fluency.

Musicians & Artists

Expand creative potential, refine tonal perception, and deepen the connection between body, voice, and expression.

Mental Wellbeing

Addresses depression, anxiety, insomnia, vertigo, tinnitus, and stress-related problems by rebalancing the nervous system.

Hearing & Sensory

Auditory processing difficulties, hypersensitivity to sound, or other sensory integration challenges.

Personal Growth

Anyone on a journey of self-awareness — this training grounds and releases a person to achieve their full potential through deeper listening.

A three-nation study commissioned by the European Union has shown that the Tomatis Method can reduce the time taken to learn a language by half, since it acclimatises the ear to the sounds, frequencies, and cadences of the new language — enabling the learner to achieve native fluency in a relatively short period.

Our Story

Meeting with Dr. and Mrs. Tomatis

The Tomatis Research Centre of the Auroville Language Laboratory is the very first centre in India to offer this method. We had the unforgettable opportunity of meeting Dr. Alfred Tomatis and his wife Mrs. Léna Tomatis in the south of France in December 2001, just before he passed away.

We received his blessings to bring the method to Auroville and India. Our centre opened in 2004 and we have, at the time of writing, completed 20 years of practice.

The overall protocol is modified and highly individualised as per the needs of each person — whether a child on the autism spectrum, an adult seeking self-development, or a musician expanding their sonic palette.

"The ear is not just an organ of hearing. It is a dynamo that charges the brain and gives it energy to communicate, to listen, to be fully present."
— Dr. Alfred Tomatis (1920–2001)

How It Works

Programs We Offer

The starting point of any Tomatis Program is the Listening Test — an assessment of how well a person listens. Based on the results, a customised program is devised for each individual. Each listening cycle consists of 3 listening blocks separated by breaks; often a fourth block is recommended.

Program Block 1 Break Block 2 Break Block 3
Language Assimilation
70 hrs total 		4 weeks — Passive Phase 2–3 week break — 3 weeks — Active Phase
Listening Training
70 hrs total 		3 weeks — Listening 5 week break 2 weeks — Listening 5 week break 2 weeks — Active
Mixed
70 hrs total		 3 weeks — Listening 5 week break 2 weeks — Listening 5 week break 2 weeks — Active
Shorter Programs 1–3 weeks as per individual need
For children with developmental delays or on the Autism spectrum: a Listening Test may not be possible at the start. In these cases, other evaluations are carried out during the first consultation, along with a detailed review of medical history and relevant reports. Several listening cycles are often necessary.
